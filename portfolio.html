3.1.6 Project


For this project, we tried to detect where the rover went lost on an alien planet. We used our knowledge of lights, gyroscopes, sound and temperature to try to identify which type of land the rover was on. We ended up choosing that the rover was in the Rocky Mountains. We thought this because our temperature graph had numerous spikes which could show how the temperatures heated up quickly. Also with the bright constant light, this is shown in our light graph where it increases immensely and then plateus, representative of the occasional clouds. A low constant howl is shown by our sound graph that stayed pretty constant. Finally, the constant winds with varied intensities is demonstrated through our gyroscope graph because it remains constant but also has a lot of differing intensities, all while staying centered around 0. This is why we believe that the rover is in the Rocky Mountains.
